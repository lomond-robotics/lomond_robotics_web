Lomond Robotics provides custom technical solutions for companies that need automation, hardware integration, practical AI implementation, or engineering prototypes.

## Automation and robotics

Design and implementation of systems that reduce repetitive manual work, improve consistency, or connect machines and processes together. This can include sensors, actuators, controllers, robotics, test rigs, and workflow automation.

## Hardware integration

Support connecting physical equipment to software, data systems, control logic, or new interfaces. This includes selecting components, wiring and control, embedded devices, data capture, commissioning, and troubleshooting.

## AI tool consultation and implementation

Advice and implementation support for using AI tools in a company setting. The focus is practical adoption: choosing useful workflows, integrating tools with existing processes, handling constraints, and making sure the result is understandable and maintainable.

## Prototyping and custom devices

Design and build support for one-off devices, fixtures, proof-of-concept systems, and engineering prototypes. 3D printing can be used when it is the right tool, but it is only one part of the prototyping and implementation process.

## Measurement and data capture

Custom devices or setups for measuring, logging, monitoring, or triggering events when off-the-shelf tools do not match the job.

## On-demand engineering support

If the problem is unusual, uncertain, or not yet fully specified, Lomond Robotics can help turn it into a clear technical plan and then build the right level of solution.
